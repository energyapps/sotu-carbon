About the Data

Data

Carbon data are aggregated from IEA’s CO2 Emissions From Fuel Combustion Highlights 2017 . Units are in Metric Tonnes.

Geography

Some countries could not be included, due to a lack of detailed information. To get a detailed understanding of why certain countries are excluded, read chapter 4 of the IEA documentation .

Source: CO2 Emissions from Fuel Combustion (2017 Edition), OECD/IEA, Paris.
